    Welcome to PT. Nasional Makmur Sejahtera

    located in Glodok District, Jakarta, Indonesia

    Let's start

    Established in 1969 and incorporated
    in 2006

    PT. Nasional Makmur Sejahtera has been known as a reliable and capable supplier to many of our valued industrial and manufacturing customers. Our headquarter and branch are located in Glodok District, a centralized area known for its distribution of tools, machineries, and industrial equipments.

    The Expertise

    Our expertise covers automotive and other related industrial equipments such as Torque Wrench, Nutrunner, Spring Balancer, Toggle Clamp, Magnetic Tools, Spray Gun, Tipped Tools, Dial Thickness Durometer, Cutting Tools, Welding Gauge, Stamp Punches, Air Tools, Measuring Tools, Air Compressors, etc. As of today, we hold sole distributorship rights for several renowned Japanese Industrial product brands (Kakuta, Kanetec, and Kowa) and are authorized dealers for similar brands (Tohnichi, Endo, Esthic, etc.).

    Strong Vision

    Over the past years, we have firmly established our ground in related areas and received the credibility of various clients from automotive assemblers such as ASTRA-DAIHATSU, ASTRA-HONDA, General Motors, HONDA PROSPECT, HINO, KAWASAKI, MITSUBISHI-FUSO KRAMA YUDHA, NISSAN, SUZUKI and YAMAHA; as well part makers such as ASTRA AUTOPARTS, ASTRA-GKD, ASTRA-MTM, DENSO, KAYABA, TOYOTA-ASNO HORIE, and TOYOTA TSUSHO. Marketwise, we are constantly seeking for partners with high quality products with competitive prices to market. In addition, we also aggressively seek expansion in current and new markets. In the future, we are planning to develop mini satellites in industrial areas around Jakarta City.
